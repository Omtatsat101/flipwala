🌐 Cross-Border Payment Information

  • GST Implications: 18% GST applies on services for Indian buyers. FlipWala provides GST-compliant invoices for all transactions.
  • Forex Rates: Currency conversion uses live mid-market rates with a 0.5% markup. Rates are locked at the time of escrow deposit.
  • FEMA Compliance: Transactions involving Indian residents comply with FEMA guidelines. LRS limits (currently $250,000/year) apply for outward remittances by Indian buyers.
  • TCS/TDS: Tax Collected at Source (TCS) of 5% applies on forex transactions above ₹7L under LRS. We provide necessary documentation for tax filing.

Regional Domain Strategy

Dominate your regional market with the right domain strategy.

🇮🇳 Why .in / .co.in Domains Matter

India is the world's largest internet market by user count. A .in domain signals local credibility, improves Google.co.in rankings, and is essential for Indian consumers who prefer local brands. .co.in is ideal for commercial entities targeting Indian buyers specifically.

  • ✓ Higher trust with Indian consumers
  • ✓ Better SEO on Google India
  • ✓ Required for some Indian payment gateways
  • ✓ Affordable: ₹500-900/year

🌎 Cross-Border Domain Strategy for NRIs

NRI entrepreneurs need domains that work across markets. Our recommended approach: primary .com for global presence, .in for Indian operations, and regional TLDs (.ae, .pk, .lk) for specific markets.

  • ✓ Unified brand across .com + .in + regional
  • ✓ Hreflang tags for multi-region SEO
  • ✓ Country-specific landing pages
  • ✓ Single SSL covering all subdomains
